Helping pets find their forever home since 1999.

At Penny’s Pet Rescue, our mission is to give every animal the chance to find their forever home by creating the perfect match between pet and person. We believe that every adoption should be built on understanding — pairing each animal’s personality, needs, and quirks with a family’s lifestyle, environment, and heart.

Located at the heart of Parramatta CBD, we are easily accessible to suburbs around Western Sydney. We are grateful to partner with Pablo's Veterinary Services.

Ethical Statement

Ethics are at the forefront of our work. We collect only necessary information, in accordance with Australian privacy laws, to support the adoption process, ensure responsible pet ownership, and keep you informed about our rescue work.

Acknowledgement of Country

Penny’s Pet Rescue acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the land on which we work and care for animals. We pay our respects to Elders past and present, and we recognise the enduring connection of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples to land, waters, community, and all living creatures.
